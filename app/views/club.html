The Club

History

West Van Royals Football Club is a West Vancouver-based soccer team established in 1976. The Royals currently play in the Vancouver Metro Soccer League (VMSL) during the Winter and the North Shore Sportsman's League (NSSL) in the Summer.

Home field

The Royals play their home games at Ambleside ' F ' grass field. Heading west down Marine Dr, turn left on 13th St and continue over the train tracks. Take a left at the 3-way onto Argyle Ave, the field will be on your left.
